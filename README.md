### 1. Value/Novelty

This project, titled Nuns Band, explores the boundaries of AI-Driven Narrative Filmmaking, moving beyond simple stylized video experiments.

**State-of-the-Art Technology Stack:**  
We integrated a matrix of leading generative AI tools, including Kling and Seedance for high-quality motion generation, Suno for AI music composition, and ChatGPT/Gemini for advanced prompt engineering.

**Original Narrative Framework:**  
We established a "Zero-Code" narrative framework to explore the conflict between modern urban pressure and surreal spiritual escape. The story follows Elias, an exhausted programmer, as he is drawn away from a reality of debt and overtime by the mysterious music of a nun band.

**Visual Language Innovation:**  
The project successfully translates text into a precise "physical visual language," establishing a Unified Dark Religious Visual Style. This aesthetic combines desolate urban settings with ritualistic symbols, creating a unique and highly recognizable original work.

### 2. Github Repository & Technical Implementation

#### Core Technical Workflow

We implemented and documented a comprehensive six-stage workflow from pre-production to final delivery:

1. **Concept Development:** Used ChatGPT/Gemini to generate visual design documents and moodboards.

2. **Prompt Engineering:** Built a structured prompt matrix: [Subject]+[Env]+[Light]+[Camera]+[Style] to ensure logical consistency across all visual assets.

3. **Asset Generation:** Leveraged tools like NanoBanana and Jimeng to generate core imagery.

4. **Keyframe Selection:** Conducted manual curation to maintain spatial logic, ensuring smooth transitions from wide shots to close-ups.

5. **Video Generation:** Applied Seedance and Kling for motion generation.

6. **Post-Production:** Utilized Premiere and CapCut for global LUT color unification and audio masking/sound design.

#### Role & Storyworld Integration

The project is deeply integrated with the required narrative assets:

- **Elias:** A young programmer trapped by reality, serving as the central perspective.
- **Valeria:** The calm and commanding leader of the nun band, symbolizing surreal temptation.

These characters extend the narrative logic of the polyu-storyworld repository, using AI to give them distinct visual identities and psychological depth.

### 3. Document Your Process (Reflection-in-action) 

#### Impact of AI Tools on the Production Pipeline

**Efficiency & Narrative Depth:**  
AI tools significantly shortened the cycle from text to image, allowing us to experiment with complex visual metaphors like "fractured identity" and "shattered transitions" through rapid iteration.

**Precise Visual Control:**  
The structured prompt matrix allowed for the precise realization of an "oppressive atmosphere" and "stage-like lighting," which would typically require significant practical resources.

#### Implications of Including AI Tools

**The Challenge of Visual Consistency:**  
Maintaining character consistency (e.g., Valeria and Elias) across AI-generated clips was a primary challenge. We addressed this through "Manual Curation" and "Global LUT Unification" to ensure a coherent dark urban aesthetic throughout the film.

**The Shift in Creative Paradigm:**  
AI tools have shifted the creator's role from "executor" to "director and curator". The "Zero-Code" approach demonstrates that in the AI era, mastering spatial logic and emotional mood becomes more critical than traditional technical maneuvers.
